What is संज्ञा? (Sangya / Noun)

▼

In English, a noun is the name of a person, place, or thing. In Hindi, we call a noun संज्ञा (sangya).

किसी व्यक्ति, वस्तु, स्थान या प्राणी के नाम को संज्ञा कहते हैं।
"The name of a person, thing, place, or animal is called a noun."
👧
क्रिसलिन
krislyn
(a person's name) — is a संज्ञा!
🏠
घर
ghar
(a place — "home") — is a संज्ञा!
🐘
हाथी
haathi
(an animal — "elephant") — is a संज्ञा!
📖
किताब
kitaab
(a thing — "book") — is a संज्ञा!

💡 Remember

If you can point to it or name it — it's a संज्ञा! People, animals, places, and things all have names. Those names are nouns.

2

The 4 Types of संज्ञा

▼

Every noun in Hindi belongs to one of four families:

👨‍👩‍👧
व्यक्ति
vyakti
Person
Names of people
राम, माँ, भाई, क्रिसलिन
🐘
प्राणी
praani
Animal / Living being
Names of animals & birds
हाथी, कुत्ता, तोता, मछली
🏙️
स्थान
sthaan
Place
Names of places
दिल्ली, भारत, स्कूल, घर
📦
वस्तु
vastu
Thing / Object
Names of objects
किताब, कुर्सी, गेंद, कार
