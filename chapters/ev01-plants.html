What Do Plants Need to Grow?

▼

Plants are living things — just like you and me! To grow healthy and strong, they need four important things:

💧

Water

From rain or watering

☀️

Sunlight

To make their food

💨

Air

Mainly carbon dioxide

🟫

Soil

For nutrients and support

🤔 Did You Know?

Without any ONE of these four things, a plant cannot survive! That's why we water plants, place them near windows, and use good soil.

All About Roots

▼

Roots grow underground. They are the hidden heroes of the plant!

What Do Roots Do?

  • Anchor the plant — hold it firmly in the soil so it doesn't fall
  • Absorb water and minerals from the soil (like drinking through a straw!)
  • Store food — some roots (like carrots and radishes) store food for the plant

Two Types of Roots

🥕

Tap Root

ONE main thick root going deep into the ground, with smaller roots branching from it.

Examples: Carrot, Radish, Turnip, Mango tree, Neem tree
🌾

Fibrous Root

MANY thin roots of the same size growing in a bunch from the base of the stem.

Examples: Grass, Wheat, Rice, Maize, Onion

🤔 Cool Fact!

When you eat a carrot, you're actually eating a ROOT! Same with radish, turnip, and beetroot. These roots store food for the plant — and we eat that food!

4

Stems and Leaves

▼

🌿 Stem

The stem is like the skeleton of a plant. It:

  • Supports leaves, flowers, and fruits
  • Carries water from roots to leaves
  • Carries food from leaves to the whole plant
  • Holds the plant up towards sunlight

Trees have thick woody stems called trunks!

🍃 Leaf

Leaves are like tiny kitchens. They:

  • Make food for the plant (called photosynthesis)
  • Breathe in carbon dioxide and breathe out oxygen
  • Release extra water as tiny drops

The green colour of leaves comes from chlorophyll!

✨ The Amazing Food-Making Magic

💧 Water + ☀️ Sunlight + 💨 Air (CO₂) = 🍃 Food for Plant

This food-making happens inside leaves, using the green chlorophyll. It's called photosynthesis!

All About Flowers

▼

Flowers are the most beautiful and colourful part of a plant — but they have an important job too!

What Do Flowers Do?

  • Their bright colours and smell attract insects like bees and butterflies
  • Insects help flowers make seeds (this is called pollination)
  • After pollination, flowers turn into fruits 🍎
  • Inside the fruit, there are seeds 🌱 that grow into new plants!

🐝 Flower → Fruit → Seed → New Plant!

The life cycle of a flowering plant: A flower is pollinated by bees → It becomes a fruit → The fruit contains seeds → Seeds are planted → They grow into new plants → Which grow more flowers!

6

Trees — Giants of the Plant World

▼

Trees are very large plants with thick, hard, woody stems called trunks. They live for many, many years — some trees live for hundreds or even thousands of years!

🍂 Deciduous Trees

These trees shed their leaves in a certain season (usually autumn/winter). New leaves grow in spring!

Examples: Mango, Neem, Oak, Maple

🌲 Evergreen Trees

These trees stay green all year round. They keep their leaves (though old ones slowly fall and new ones grow).

Examples: Pine, Cedar, Coconut, Banyan

🌍 Let's Protect Plants!

Plants give us SO much, but we must take care of them too! We can: water plants, plant new trees, not pluck flowers unnecessarily, and protect forests. Every plant matters!

8

Amazing & Unusual Plants (Enrichment)

▼

Some plants are really special! Let's meet a few:

🪴 Cactus

Lives in deserts with very little water. Its leaves are tiny spines so water doesn't escape. Its thick stem stores water!

🌸 Lotus

Grows in water! Its long stem holds it up above the water surface.

🌵 Venus Flytrap

This plant eats INSECTS! It traps bugs when they land on its special leaves.

🌴 Banyan Tree

This tree grows EXTRA roots from its branches down to the ground — one tree can look like a whole forest!

🎋 Bamboo

The fastest-growing plant in the world! Some types grow almost 1 metre in a single day!

🌊 Mangroves

Trees that grow with their roots partly in salty sea water. Their roots stick up above the water!
