1. गुठली की बुआई का क्रम · Order of Planting

2. फल · अनाज · सब्ज़ी · Sort into Baskets

Tap a word, then tap the right basket. Fruits go in 🥭, grains in 🌾, vegetables in 🥕.

3. विशेषता · Match the Describing Word

In the story, each describing word goes with one thing. मीठे goes with… आम (sweet mangoes)! Find the right friend for each word below.

4. काम वाले शब्द या नाम वाले शब्द?

Look at each word from the story. Is it a doing word (काम / verb) or a naming word (नाम / noun)? Tap the right answer.

5. गुठली वाले फल या बिना गुठली वाले फल?

A गुठली is a big, hard pit (like a mango pit). Some fruits have one big pit, others don't. Sort these fruits!
