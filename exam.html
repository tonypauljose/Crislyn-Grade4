📝 Exam Mode

25 questions · 30-minute timer · Pick a chapter and give it a try!

📋 How the exam works

  • 25 questions — a mix of multiple-choice, true/false and fill-in-the-blank.
  • 30-minute timer at the top. The exam auto-submits when it hits 0:00.
  • You can jump between questions, mark some for review, and change your answers any time before you submit.
  • Nothing is graded until you click Submit. Then you'll see your marks out of 100, with a friendly review of every question.
  • Optional: at submit-time you can enter a parent's email and get the result by email — leave blank to skip.
